The PROSTARS Method™

Five inner skills. Three visible proofs.

Movement is the delivery tool, not the product. Every session builds one of five inner-readiness traits — and produces one of three kinds of proof a parent can actually see.

P · Presence, R · Resilience, O · Observation, S · Social Connection, T · Trying

The inner layer — what we actually teach, session by session. This is the part a sports class skips.

A · Achievement, R · Respect, S · Strength

The outer layer — the badges, the noticeable behaviour, the physical competence. The proof you get at pickup.
